Why I like it

An absolute masterpiece of animation and storytelling. This is by far my absolute favourite movie. Not only am I in love with the art style, but the story is engaging and heartfelt. Here are a few reasons why it stands out for me:

  • Revolutionary Animation: The comic art-style they developed for this movie is completely new and unique. Every frame could be a comic book panel.
  • Emotional Depth: The story engages you on an emotional level. The romance between Miles and Gwen is beautifully portrayed and it shows how difficult life is as a teenager.
  • Diverse Characters: Characters are very well developed, each with their own distinct personalities and backgrounds.
  • Attention to detail: the animators put so much detail into each character and scene that every character from every universe has its own unique look and style with emotions linked to them.
  • Relatable Themes: Belonging and destiny are important themes in the movie. Miles' journey of finding his own path while dealing with expectations is incredibly relatable and moving
