Exploring the World of Action Movies

What is an action Movie?

Action movies are movies that have big stunts, explosion, tension and energy that keep the viewer engaged. Good Visual Effects and Sound effects is what adds a lot to the movie too.

Action movies are often about:

  • Life / Death Situation
  • hero/ villain dynamics
  • justice or revenge
  • physicall challnges
"Success isn't always about greatness. It's about consistency. Consistent hard work leads to success. Greatness will come" — Dwayne Johnson, Actor

Action Stunt Choreography

Stunts are what most movies are remembered for. Actors, in most cases the main actor, gets a stunt double to perform dangerous scenes. From high speed cars chases, to buildings that are collapsing, or jumps from very high, the stunt double has to perform everything. Safetly is always the number one priority when it comes to stunts.

Visual Effects & Cinematography

Modern movies are getting better and better at blending reality with computer generated imagery. CGI makes it possible to create scenes that are not possible to film in real life. VFX teams use various techniques to bring these scenes to life. Greenscreen is a very common and known technique to seperate the background from the actors. Camera work, lighting compositions and angles are used to create better looking compositions. This is all what comes together in filmmaking.

"The best visual effects are the ones audiences don't realize are effects. We blend the real with the digital to create moments that feel authentic and exhilarating." — VFX Industry Professional

Sound Design

Sound design is what makes an action movie a true action movie. Some people even say sound is as important as the visual side of the movie. From explosion, gunfire and collision impacts to simple filmtrack music. Audio is what makes you feel the action sequence. Sound designers create tension and expand the excitement.
