Exploring the World of Action Movies

What is an action Movie?

Action movies are movies that have big stunts, explosion, tension and energy that keep the viewer engaged. Good Visual Effects and Sound effects is what adds a lot to the movie too.

Action movies are often about:

  • Life / Death Situation
  • hero/ villain dynamics
  • justice or revenge
  • physicall challnges
