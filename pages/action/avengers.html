Why I like it

One of my favourite films, and similarly to 'Star Wars', it's one of those movies where the universe and all the backstories are so interesting. Here are a few reasons why it stands out for me:

  • The Team Dynamic: It balances out each superhero, which makes it a movie of unity. All characters are so different, but they make a good team
  • Iconic Characters: It's just such a classic movie that I had to include it here. My favourite character is and was always Iron Man. He has no real powers, he is just smart and rich
