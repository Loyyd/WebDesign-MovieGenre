Why I like it

'The Matrix' is one of my favoirite films for me, and it's one of those movies where I have to think about it long after its watched. The reason is becuase I realy like movies that are about alternative realities and big concepts. Here are a few reasons why it stands out for me:

  • Deep Philosophical Themes: The movie questions the existence of reality itself and brings a lot of topics in such as complex ideas of religion or philosophy.
  • Iconic Style: You really like the characters quickly and their style is very memorable. Sunglases and leather coats.
