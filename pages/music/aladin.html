Why I like it

  • Themeatic Music: The music in this Movie captures the easter environment through a blend of orchestral and Arabic musical features. These include things like pluced strings and use of flattened sevenths in songs such as Arabian Nights
  • Beautiful Animation Aladdin was made during Disneys animation golden age. The film was animated on cell sheets which brings life and colour into every scene

    • My Favourite Song

    Alan Wenken - Prince Ali

    This is my favourite song from Aladdin. It is a jaunty showtune that keeps an energy throughout. No two verses are the same with different instruments and metre changes throughout. The background singers represent different people in the crowd tying the song to the environment in a creative way.
