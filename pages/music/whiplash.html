Why I like it

  • Tribute To Jazz: The film has an incredible love for the genre of Jazz. Everything from the complexity of the music itself to the intense percerverence it takes to become a successful jazz musician is felt in every scene of this film. The utter love for the genre makes this film one of my favourites
  • Psychological Impact: The Film dives deep into the short and log term effects of dedicating your whole life to such a daunting undertaking as studying Jazz. Characters feel overwhelmed constantly which is exhibited audibly through the face pace of each track. In this scene the song is constantly stopped and started and wiht every repitition the agitation of both Fletcher and Andrew is palpable.
