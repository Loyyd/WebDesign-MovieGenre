Whiplash follows Andrew. A drummer who is scouted by the orchestra conductor Fletcher who seeks to turn Andrew Neiman into the most incredible drummer there is.

Why I like it

Tribute To Jazz: The film has an incredible love for the genre of Jazz. Everything from the complexity of the music itself to the intense percerverence it takes to become a successful jazz musician is felt in every scene of this film. The utter love for the genre makes this film one of my favourites

Psychological Impact: The Film dives deep into the short and long term effects of dedicating your whole life to such a daunting undertaking as studying Jazz. Characters feel overwhelmed constantly which is exhibited audibly through the face pace of each track. In this scene the song is constantly stopped and started and with every repitition the agitation of both Fletcher and Andrew is palpable.

My Favourite Song

Tim Simonec - Upswingin'

This is my favourite song from Whiplash. It plays during the final scene, the tense flow of the song shows beautifully the tension that has been built between Fletcher and Andrew. Aside from that, I am a sucker for an abnormal metre Jazz piece.
