A Quiet Place (2018) is a post-apocalyptic horror film directed by John Krasinski. It follows a family struggling to survive in a world overrun by deadly creatures that hunt by sound. Forced to live in near-total silence, they communicate through sign language and take extreme measures to avoid making noise. When the mother becomes pregnant, the family faces their most dangerous challenge yet. The film is acclaimed for its tense atmosphere, emotional depth, and innovative use of sound and silence.
