Why It's Good

  • Pioneer of the Slasher Genre: Friday the 13th helped define the "slasher formula" a group of young people isolated in a remote location, being hunted by an unseen killer. This formula influenced countless horror films throughout the 1980s.
  • Birth of a Horror Icon: Although Jason Voorhees only appears briefly in this first film, he would go on to become one of horror‚Äôs most legendary villains, shaping pop culture for decades.
  • Independent Success: Made on a low budget, it became a massive box-office hit, proving that independent horror films could be both profitable and culturally powerful.
  • Lasting Legacy: The movie inspired numerous sequels, remakes, and even video games turning Friday the 13th into one of the longest-running horror franchises in film history.
