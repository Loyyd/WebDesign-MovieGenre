Tenet is a mind-bending sci-fi thriller where the protagonist learns to manipulate time using an ability known as "Inversion" to prevent World War III. The movie also explores concepts of time loops, and nonlinear nature of events.

Why It's Worth Watching

  • Innovative Action sequences: The film is known for its "inverted" action, where objects nad people move backward through time, which creates unique and mind-bending set pieces.
  • Complex & thought provoking plot: Tenet is among the most intricate and convoluted pieces of work in Christopher Nolan's career. The complexity of the film often leaves the viewer in deep thought, which is a very rewarding feeling to Christopher Nolan.
  • Cinematic spectacle: The film features exotic locations, a high level production value making it a large-scale blockbuster designed for the biggest screnes possible.
