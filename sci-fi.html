What is a Sci-Fi Movie?

Sci-Fi movies are movies that have big stunts, explosion, tension and energy that keep the viewer engaged. Good Visual Effects and Sound effects is what adds a lot to the movie too.

Sci-Fi movies cover concepts such as:

  • Philosophical concepts
  • Genetic Concepts and Human Evolution
  • Time Travel and parallel universes
  • Impeccable storytelling
  • Alien Life and Technology
