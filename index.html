Horror Genre

▶ Horror Movies

What is Horror?

I chose the horror genre because I think it’s one of the most exciting and impactful genres out there. Horror has a way of grabbing people’s attention instantly and pulling them into the story through fear, suspense, and the unknown. I love how it can make audiences feel real emotions like shock, tension and adrenaline that other genres don’t always reach. Horror also sticks with people, even after the scene is over, they’re still thinking about it. To me, that lasting effect is what makes the genre so powerful and why I wanted to base my section around it.
