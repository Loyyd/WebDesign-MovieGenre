▶ Sci-Fi Movies

What are Sci-Fi Movies?

The world of Sci-Fi is one with amazing storytelling, visual effects, and otherworldly aspects. All of these features make for a movie that keeps the viewer hooked and on the edge of their seat. Personally, I love sci-fi because I can get lost in a world where the impossible feels possible. I would always recommend watching Sci-Fi, preferably Christopher Nolan movies because they often leave you in thought which normal movies fail to do.

Parikshit Hari

▶ Horror Movies

What is Horror?

A horror movie is a film genre that aims to frighten, disturb, or unsettle viewers. It often features supernatural forces, monsters, killers, or psychological fears, building suspense and tension to keep the audience on edge.

Alexandru Fechete
